Counting game
c.f. Cyanland@Ycool

Sometimes I think, why we don't play the animate cards, because it would not be thicker than a card of mahjong.
But, it's still not easy to invent a game.
Inspired by the game of 24, I tried to make something like this...

Type of card:

Basic card: one can use it once as a number or an operator as it's printed. The numbers are only 0 to 20, in total there're 100 cards.

Trigonometric card: one can use it once as a number or an operator as it's printed, or the trigonometric values of the number. The numbers are 0, 5, 10, ..., 90, in total there're 38 cards.

Exponential card: one can use it once as a number or an operator as it's printed, or operator when a=number. The numbers are 0 to 20. in total there're 40 cards.

Question card: The numbers are 0 to 20. in total there're 100 cards.

Choice card: there's 3 number of choice, there're 20 cards.

Chrono card: Every minute it's added by 1, or as the number of pi or e. There're 20 cards.

Insane card: the number changes randomly, as well as the rhytm of change, but one can use it as any operator. There're 20 cards.

Type of game:

Basic game: without the trigonometric cards and exponential cards, all of people divide equally the cards besides the question cards. After one show out a question card, the first one who gives the answer with his cards wins the question card, and pick 2 card from each one. who wins the most of question card win the game.

Expert game: one plays with all the cards.

We could use the solar energy, for there's no place for a battery.

In addition, I never play this game. ^_^|
