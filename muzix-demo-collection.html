Classical, game and animation music as current style, ton of unfinished MIDI chips with some brilliant ideas, the most outstanding ones are like « Firework » and « Dark Blue » (Inconveniently the MIDI converter that I use doesn't give the same sound as when I compose, sorry for Firework). ...

不孤单 (2014)
During this first period of creation, like everyone else, pop is the style while Yusheng Zhang (张雨生) is the idol. Gradually finding the voice destroyed by a incorrect way of singing, creation once stopped. « 我相信 » the only song with lyrics written during those frustrating univ days, and the best song I've ever written. As is a memorial of Yusheng, it's starting with a quite high pitch, and cross over 2 octaves, a real challenge. After that I'm convinced that I don't have an ability to sing out whatever I wrote. That eventually made me focus more on music itself...
