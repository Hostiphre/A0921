Above Helium （希里亚姆的传说）
Type: Novel; Language: Chinese
In a dark and mysterious universe, a blue and green sphere called the Earth is spinning slowly. In the fertile nature of the southern hemisphere, human and dinosaurs share a carefree life. But with the rapid development of the Xenon civilization on northern hemisphere, the war about resources becomes inevitable. At the end of the war against Helium, the son of the Xenon army's head, Yutian, suddenly left the barracks alone, to find somewhere "of 11 years ago". During the journey, Yutian met the mysterious Helium boy, Yuan, the Xenon "wanted" scientist, Xue Shu, a Neon walking-dead, Roy, and a dinaseur with only the lower half, Ron, etc. A long story about the love and hartred between Helium and Xenon people is then discovered... (The original version of AH is RAR (Roy and Ron), an unfinished manga in 2003.)
