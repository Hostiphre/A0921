Hostiphre (also as 野草, Hesper) is the pseudonym of Ariane Lu Ding (丁蕗), post-doc at the Quantum physics department of the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics of the Charles University in Prague. "Ariane" and "Lu" are both first names given by parents, but the former remains unofficial. "Hostiphre" is a letter rearrangement of “Sephiroth”, which takes the meaning of "hostility-free", kind of response to Sephiroth's tragedy and the whole world's hatred.

Ding was born and grew up in Zhong Guan Cun (Beijing, China); then obtained her master and PhD degree in France before moved to Prague. Her parents were married on the Chinese national day (1 Oct. 1987) and she was born in the French national day (14 July 1988). She speaks Chinese, French, English and a bit of (Czech, German and Japonese).
